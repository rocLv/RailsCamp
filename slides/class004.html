  • 类变量： ‘@@’开始， 如@@foo
  • 全局变量： ‘$’开始， 如$:

变量作用域

A variable's scope determines where in a program a variable is available for use.

Ruby中以block来隔离作用域

PS: Inner scope can access variables initialized in an outer scope, but not vice versa.

1 a = 5             # variable is initialized in the outer scope
2 
3 3.times do |n|    # method invocation with a block
4   a = 3           # is a accessible here, in an inner scope?
5 end
6 
7 puts a
